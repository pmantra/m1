Domestic shipping and international travel kits

Domestic Travel

Pump + Post Kit

Mail your breast milk back home with our simple domestic shipping kit. Share your travel details and your kits will be waiting at your accommodations.

  • Transports up to 36 oz. of milk
  • Overnight shipping within the U.S. and up to 72 hours refrigeration in case of delays
  • (6) tamper-proof breast milk bags

Domestic Travel

Pump + Carry Packs

Our security-friendly foam ice packs are waterless, making them great for short trips. Pack them in your own cooler and bring your milk on board domestic flights.

  • TSA-friendly foam ice packs (cooler not included)
  • Keeps milk cold longer than gel or water ice packs
  • Fits easily in most travel coolers
  • Reusable
International Travel

Pump + Check Kit

Carry your milk home from abroad instead of getting stuck shipping through customs. Fill up your kit and check it in your luggage during international travel.

  • Packs up to 270 oz. of frozen milk (about 9 days worth)
  • Keeps milk frozen up to 24 hours
  • Durable Coleman cooler with antimicrobial liner
  • (9) foam cooling packs
  • Reusable
