Our values

We built Maven on the belief that healthcare is a necessity, not a luxury, and every woman and family deserves access to high-quality care.

Healthcare should be profoundly human

In-person, doctors spend 8 minutes or less with patients. Technology can improve our lives by providing an immediate, direct, and continuous line of communication between patients and providers.

Great care is whole-person care

That means providing access to physical, emotional, and lifestyle care—for all people. Not just doctors, but nurse practitioners, therapists, LGBTQ-friendly practitioners, and more.

Better care for women is a social imperative

Menstruation, contraception, fertility, postpartum depression, miscarriage—all remain taboo. Better healthcare brings women’s health out of the shadows and brings all of us closer to equality.

Our mission

Maven empowers women and families to take care and take on the world with access to high-quality healthcare and modern family benefits for all.
