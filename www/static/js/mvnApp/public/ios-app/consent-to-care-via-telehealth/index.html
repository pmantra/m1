Consent to Services via Coaching

I understand that the virtual appointment I have requested is not for medical services, such as medical assessment, diagnosis, or treatment. My intent in scheduling the virtual appointment is to obtain general information, education, coaching, guidance, and/or referrals and not to replace the care I am receiving from my medical providers.

I agree to receive this virtual appointment with the professional whom I selected. I understand that the professional providing the identified services to me and utilizing the Maven platform through the Maven Site, as defined in the Maven Terms of Use, may be remotely located from my location.

The scope of services will be at the sole discretion of the professional from whom I am receiving this service, but will be limited to general information, education, coaching, guidance, and/or referrals. I acknowledge and understand that any assessment, diagnosis, treatment, or prescription is excluded from the scope of services. The professional will determine whether or not the questions or concerns I have are appropriate for the limited scope of this encounter via the Site. I consent to receive such services and to speak to a non-Maven health care practitioner before taking any action in reliance on such services.

I also agree and authorize my professional to release information regarding the consultation to Maven consistent with the Terms of Use set forth on the Site.
A virtual appointment means that my services are provided, as applicable (A) by a professional at a distant location using interactive video and/or audio conferencing in real time or (B) with the aid of exchanged digital images, by an asynchronous remote consultation.

I also understand that:

  • The services provided by the professional are educational in nature and that I should consult with my healthcare provider(s) if I need any medical services, including medical assessment, diagnosis, or treatment. I understand this may require travel and may need to be arranged urgently if an urgent need arises
  • It is not the purpose of this service to create a patient/provider relationship.
  • The same confidentiality and privacy protections that apply to my Maven’s services also apply to these services.
  • I have access to all of my health and wellness information pertaining to the services in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

I have read this document carefully, and all my questions were answered to my satisfaction. I hereby consent to participate in the virtual appointment with my selected professional using the Site pursuant to the terms, conditions, standards, and requirements referenced at the Site.

Consent to Services via Telehealth

I agree to receive this health care service provided by the health care practitioner(s) whom I selected. As a telehealth service, I understand that the health care practitioner(s), providing the identified services to me and utilizing the Maven telehealth platform through the Maven Site as defined in the Maven Terms of Use, is(are) located in location(s) remote from my location.

The scope of care will be at the sole discretion of the health care practitioner(s) from whom I am receiving this service, with no guarantee of diagnosis, treatment, or prescription. The health care practitioner(s) will determine whether or not the condition being diagnosed and/or treated is appropriate for a telehealth encounter via the Site.

I also agree and authorize my health care practitioner(s) to release information regarding the telehealth visit to Maven consistent with the Terms of Use set forth on the Site.

A telehealth service means that my health care services are provided, as applicable (A) by practitioner(s) at a distant location using interactive video and/or audio conferencing in real time or (B) with the aid of exchanged digital images of my condition, by an asynchronous remote evaluation and consultation.

I also understand that:

  • I may have to travel to see a health care practitioner in–person for certain diagnosis and treatment matters.
  • The same confidentiality and privacy protections that apply to my other health care services also apply to these telehealth services.
  • I have access to all of my health and wellness information pertaining to the telehealth services in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.
  • I may need to see an appropriately trained health care professional in-person immediately after the telehealth service if an urgent need arises.
  • My health care information may be shared with other individuals for treatment, payment and health care operations purposes.

I have read this document carefully, and all my questions were answered to my satisfaction. I hereby consent to participate in the telehealth visit with my selected health care practitioner(s) using the Site pursuant to the terms, conditions, standards, and requirements referenced at the Site.
