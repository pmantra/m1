Two ways to join us

If you’re invited to join Maven, you’re in great company! Only one third of applicants are invited into our network. See patients on Maven’s platform, get patients referred to your in-person practice, and join a community of exceptional practitioners.

Maven Diamond

Expand your practice on Maven’s proprietary, secure telehealth platform: MPractice. As a Maven Diamond Practitioner, you’ll meet with patients through video chat and private messaging on Maven, with the option of in-person referrals as well. Once you’ve established a record of delivering excellent digital care, you’ll be eligible to apply for an on-staff position with guaranteed hours and pay.


Maven Platinum

Apply to be a Maven Platinum Practitioner and receive in-person only referrals. When patients receive your name as a Maven-recommended provider, they know they’re getting a high-quality referral. You will not provide care via telemedicine or receive payments through Maven, but you’re welcome to apply to upgrade to Diamond at any time.

How Maven works

  • 1. Apply

    Our practitioner advisory committee will evaluate your knowledge, skills, and experience through a multi-step process that includes a resume review and license verification, reference checks, a timed assessment, and a video interview.

  • 2. Set your schedule

    Maven was designed with busy practitioners in mind—you set your own schedule (down to the minute!) and patients can book with you when you’re available.

  • 3. Connect with patients and build relationships

    See patients via video chat on your mobile device, then E-prescribe and document easily and securely via Maven’s Session Notes on your laptop. Patients can follow-up via private messaging, and you can participate in our Community Forum anytime.

  • 4. Deliver coordinated care

    Maven is passionate about providing transparent and coordinated care by allowing patients to share their session notes across practitioners and facilitating in-person referrals and care coordination whenever needed.
