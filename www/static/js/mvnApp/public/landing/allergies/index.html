What are seasonal allergies?

  • Also known as hay fever or allergic rhinitis
  • Sneezing, coughing, and itchy eyes/nose, only during certain times of the year
  • Mimic common cold symptoms
  • Commonly triggered by grass, pollen, and mold
  • Your body’s reaction to a harmless environmental substance

Symptoms of seasonal allergies

  • Nasal congestion
  • Sneezing
  • Clear runny nose
  • Itchy eyes and nose
  • Sinus pressure
  • Postnasal dripping
  • Loss of smell and taste

Treatments for seasonal allergies and how Maven can help

  • Antihistamines. These over the counter medications can help reduce the effects of seasonal allergies by blocking the receptors of the allergy particles.
  • Decongestants. While sometimes available over the counter, a Maven practitioner can prescribe stronger versions of these mediations which can reduce nasal congestion.
  • Use a dehumidifier at home. Using a dehumidifier can reduce the effects of seasonal allergies. Speak with a Maven practitioner about changes you can make to your living environment to improve your allergy conditions.
